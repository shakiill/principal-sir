About me

I am Mohammed Shakhawat Hossain working as a Principal and Associate Professor at Daffodil Institute of IT (DIIT), completed my SSC from Comilla High School and HSC from Comilla Victoria Govt. College. After that I enrolled in University of Dhaka and completed my bachelor, post graduation(Major in Banking) and MBA(Major in Marketing) under the faculty of business studies. Currently I am a research fellow of DBA at Institute of Business Administration(IBA), University of Dhaka .

My research topic is Practices of Blue Ocean Strategies in the Mobile Financial Sector of Bangladesh-Study of bKash. Doing research is my passion and I conduct research class in bachelor and masters program at DIIT.

However in my free time I like to meet and invite people to make them understand the reality of life and religion.
